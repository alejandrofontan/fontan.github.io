Student Projects

I have supervised over 45 students in individual projects and theses. I regularly upload project proposals and also accept proactive applications of highly motivated students. If you are interested in working on a project in the fields of robotic scene understanding, active perception, or long-term and continual learning, please send a short CV and transcript to lschmid@mit.edu. Please ensure that you are accepted/eligible for an MIT project first.
